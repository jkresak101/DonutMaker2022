About The Bakery

Fred the Baker Baking Company has been making hot, fresh donuts for the last 57 years. Fred traveled the world over, where once he met real Giants. Fred has a secret recipe that he stole from the giants living deep in the Black Forest of Germany. After them accepting him as one of their own, he betrayed them. Hurt, the Giants went into eternal hiding from the shame that their special donuts are no more. Fred has decided he is ready to die and wants his stolen donuts to stay alive.
